Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Prostavax (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit this website, interact with our emails, or purchase our products.

Information We Collect

We collect two kinds of information:

  • Information you provide directly. Examples include your name, email address, shipping details, and payment method when you place an order or submit a customer support inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process orders, manage accounts, and provide customer support
  • Send product updates, promotional messages, and transactional communications
  • Improve our website, marketing, and product experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or potential fraud

Cookies and Tracking

Cookies and similar technologies help us remember your preferences, understand how visitors use the site, and personalize offers. You can update your browser settings to refuse cookies, although some site features may not function as intended.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell customer information. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist with order fulfillment, payment processing, email delivery, analytics, and customer support. These partners are authorized to use your information only as necessary to provide services to Prostavax. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your Choices

You may opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in our messages. You may also request to update or delete your personal information by contacting us at support@prostavax.com.

Region-Specific Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you reside in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your personal data, or object to certain processing.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). California residents may request details about personal information collected and shared, opt out of certain data practices, and request deletion of their personal information.

To exercise any privacy rights, email privacy@prostavax.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

Children’s Privacy

Our website and products are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us so we can delete it.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
